UX Designer

What We're Up To

RBCA is a product-driven creative agency that works with a range of startups, small businesses, and big brands to create compelling, successful, industry-leading apps and websites. Our mission is to help entrepreneurs, whether solo or part of a thousand-person company, build and scale their business. We help people perfect and realize their visions for digital products. Other developers just churn out what a client asks for, while we strive to truly understand the problems our clients are aiming to solve and stop at nothing to build solid products that present the optimal solution. We are a lean, tenacious machine powered by the sharpest, most passionate people we could find.

Requirements

  • Demonstrable UX design thinking across a portfolio of digital products
  • Proven Mobile UI experience
  • Experience working from product requirements to create user flows and wireframes
  • Ability to create prototypes to communicate design direction
  • Ability to solve problems creatively and efficiently
  • Solid understanding of design for iOS platform
  • Experience working with developers on design implementation
  • Strong collaborative and communication skills
  • At least 3* years of professional experience in digital product design

Nice to Have

  • Visual design skills
  • Animation/motion skills
  • Service design thinking
  • Experience in health-tracking or wellness-coaching style products

Perks and Salary

  • 21 days holiday (excluding bank holidays)
  • Annual performance bonuses
  • Biannual meetups/trips abroad
  • 27" iMac to work from when in the office
  • Your very own Apple watch (sports edition after successful 3-month probationary period)
  • Monthly team dinners and activities
